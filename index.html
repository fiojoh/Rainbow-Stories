What I do

I feel passionately about the importance of promoting oral literacy in children - starting as young as possible to help them in the development of their reading and writing. However, what I have loved most is watching the children find their voices, develop their imagination and how their confidence grows with public speaking and performance the more opportunity they have to share their stories with their peers and adults.
